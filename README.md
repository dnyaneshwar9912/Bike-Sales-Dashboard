## 📊 Project Overview
An interactive **Excel Dashboard** built to analyze **113,036 bike sales transactions** from 2011 to 2016 across 6 countries.

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze revenue, profit, and sales trends and provide actionable business insights using Microsoft Excel.

## Key Insights
- **United States** is the highest revenue-generating country: **$27.98 Million** (32.7% of total revenue)
- **Bikes** category contributes the highest profit: **$20.52 Million** (63.7% of total profit)
- Australia and United States together account for **over 57%** of total revenue
- Strong seasonal peaks observed in **July, November, and December**

## 🛠️ Features
- Interactive **Slicers** (Year, Country, Product Category, Customer Gender)
- KPI Cards (Total Revenue, Total Orders, Total Profit, Top Country)
- Revenue Analysis by Country
- Profit Analysis by Product Category
- Monthly Revenue Trend (2011–2016)
